About Us

Welcome to our Election Assistance System! Our project aims to revolutionize the voter registration process by introducing an advanced system that utilizes secure face recognition technology right at the polling booth. With our efficient face recognition feature, we aim to eliminate fraudulent voting practices and enhance the overall voting experience for citizens. By providing a paper-free and secure solution, we contribute to a more sustainable and transparent electoral process.

At Your Service

Election Result

On this website, you can view the election results for each constituency. It provides the accurate voting count of each candidate along with their details. The results are published by authorized officers to ensure correctness and transparency.
Click the button below to view the election result.

View Result

Count In Polling Booth

Use this website to check the crowd count in your polling station. An authorized officer at the Polling booth continuously updates the crowd count. To check the count, simply enter your polling booth number. It also provides the total number of voters in your booth.
Click the button below to check the count.

Crowd In Booth

Check Enrollment

If you are enrolled in the voter list through this Election Assistance System, you can check your enrollment status by entering your Aadhar ID. Once verified, you will receive a voter slip containing your details, as well as the polling station number and location for your convenience.

View Slip

Registration

If you are already enrolled in the voter list, you can register here in the new Election Assistance System. Provide your details, including a recent photograph, for registration.Please ensure to enter valid and accurate details during the registration process.

Add Details

Enroll in voter list

If you are not enrolled in the voter list, you can register here in the new Election Assistance System. Upload your details and required documents for verification. The authorized officer will review and verify the details you submitted. After the verification procedure, you will be added to the voter list and officially enrolled as a voter. Please ensure to provide valid and accurate information during the enrollment process.
Click the "Enroll" button below to begin the enrollment process.

Enroll

Counting Officer Login

As a Counting Officer, you can log in here to update the crowd count in the polling booth. Use your credentials to access the system and provide real-time updates on the number of people present at the polling station.
